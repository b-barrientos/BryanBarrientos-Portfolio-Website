    My journey into the world of software development started during my formative years in high school. Enrolling in my first AP Computer Science course and immersing myself in programs like EAST, I discovered a profound fascination with software and technology. Experiencing the boundless possibilities it offers, I embarked on a path that led me to pursue a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science at the University of Central Arkansas.

    During my academic tenure, I delved deep into the foundational principles of Computer Science, refining my skills and expanding my knowledge base. Complementing my formal education, I seized every opportunity to explore the practical realm of software engineering through internships and full-time positions. It was through these experiences that I ventured into mobile development, specializing in Android Native applications.

    Over the years, I've embraced the versatility of wearing multiple hats and navigating diverse tech stacks. Each role and project has enriched my expertise and fostered my passion for continuous growth. As I continue to embark on this journey, I am looking forward to expanding my skill sets and contributing to innovative endeavors in the ever-evolving landscape of software development.
